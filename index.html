NettoDrive Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how users of the NettoDrive application can request deletion of their account and associated data.

Steps to request account deletion

  1. Send an email requesting account deletion.
  2. Use the email address associated with your NettoDrive account.
  3. Send the request to: support@nettodrive.com

Once the request is received, the NettoDrive team will process the deletion request.

Data that will be deleted

  • User email address
  • Account information
  • Any data associated with the user account within the NettoDrive application

Data retention

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days. After this period, the associated user data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion or your data, please contact:

support@nettodrive.com
